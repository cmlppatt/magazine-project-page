Where's The Inspo?

Magazine Design

December 2024

" My drawing was not a picture of a hat. It was a picture of a boa
constrictor digesting an elephant... The grown-ups were not able
to understand it... They always need to have things explained. "

- Antoine de Saint-Exupéry in The Little Prince, page 4, 1943

Design does not have to be perfect or clean or minimalist. This project is a reflection of my design philosophy and inspired by Saint-Exupéry, how to have fun with design. The graphics are bold and without meaning, embracing the randomness of childlike imagination. Topics discussed include design uses in everyday life, my progression througout my first year at university and my future plans.
